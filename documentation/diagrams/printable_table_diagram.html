Relationship Summary

user 1 -> 0..1 employee

user 1 -> many audit_log

customer 1 -> many sales_order

customer 1 -> many complaint

employee 1 -> many sales_order

employee 1 -> many complaint

employee 1 -> many financial_record

employee 1 -> many budget

employee 1 -> many delivery as driver

employee 1 -> many delivery as manager

employee 1 -> many attendance

employee 1 -> many leave_request

employee 1 -> many payroll

supplier 0..1 -> many financial_record

product 1 -> many stock_batch

product 1 -> many order_item

sales_order 1 -> many order_item

sales_order 1 -> 0..1 delivery

vehicle 1 -> many delivery

delivery 1 -> 0..1 delivery_proof

Key Notes

  • PK = primary key, FK = foreign key, UK = unique key.
  • Several weak tables use composite primary keys, including order items, stock batches, attendance, leave requests, payroll, and audit logs.
  • Each sales order can have at most one delivery because delivery.order_id is unique.
  • Each delivery can have at most one proof record because delivery_proof(order_id, delivery_id) is unique.
